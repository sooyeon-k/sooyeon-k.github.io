Graduate Data Corps Fellow

During my time as a Graduate Data Corps Fellow with TenStrands, I contributed to the analysis of over 1,000 California school district policies, focusing on environmental policy and zero waste initiatives. I applied regression models and NLP techniques to uncover correlations between the strength of environmental language in policy documents and higher action scores, with an observed improvement of up to 15%. I also found that student-led initiatives increased tri-bin adoption odds by 4.5 times, and that every +1% of Democratic votes significantly raised the odds of adopting zero waste policies. My analysis also revealed that zero waste adoption is most prevalent in high-income, left-leaning, urban, and coastal districts.
