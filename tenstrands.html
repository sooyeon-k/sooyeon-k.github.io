Project Presentations

Spring 2025 Project

Focused specifically on zero waste adoption patterns across California school districts. We found that student engagement, district income, and political leaning were major predictors of tri-bin implementation and sustainability actions, with student-led initiatives increasing tri-bin adoption odds by 4.5x.



Winter 2024 Project

Analyzed over 1,000 California school district policies to explore how the strength of environmental language correlates with actual climate action outcomes. Using regression models, sentiment analysis, and text similarity techniques, we found that stronger, proactive language was modestly associated with higher environmental action scores across districts.
