RSSP Data Science Intern

As a Data Science Intern at UC Berkeley’s Chief Operating Officer’s Office, I designed and deployed over 10 Tableau dashboards to visualize key operational metrics such as labor, occupancy, and maintenance trends. I analyzed over 1,500 work orders from University Albany Village to identify delays, reduce task times, and flag high-labor cost areas. Additionally, I automated the log validation process using NLP techniques, reducing manual review time by 60%, and investigated labor schedule variances across 30+ operational units, revealing discrepancies that led to policy updates. I also developed an interactive Streamlit dashboard to forecast housing occupancy using historical data to support long-term planning and resource allocation.
